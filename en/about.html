ABOUT US

Shandong Olibang

Shandong Olibang is a modern enterprise integrating R&D, production, and operation, specializing in the production of Bisphenol-A epoxy resin. Located in Yangkou Chemical Industrial Park, Shouguang City, Weifang, Shandong Province.

Established in 2023 with an annual capacity of 20,000 tons, our products are exported to 20+ countries and regions worldwide. The company is ISO certified and committed to providing high-quality resin raw materials for coatings, anti-corrosion, composite materials, and electronic insulation industries.
