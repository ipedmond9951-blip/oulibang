Applications & Features

Epoxy Resin E-20 (601) features strong adhesion, widely used in adhesives, anti-corrosion coatings, zinc-rich primers, floor coatings, decorative coatings and varnishes.

Excellent adhesion, good flexibility and impact resistance, outstanding chemical resistance, solvent resistance and storage stability.
