Epoxy Resin E-12 (604) - Alternative

Epoxy Resin E-12 (604) is used in pure epoxy and epoxy-polyester powder coatings, anti-corrosion coatings, and insulation coatings. Features excellent electrostatic spraying performance and outstanding anti-corrosion and chemical resistance.

Applications & Features

Epoxy Resin E-12 (604) is used in anti-corrosion coatings, insulation coatings and other applications.

Excellent electrostatic spraying performance and outstanding anti-corrosion and chemical resistance, meeting requirements for glossy, matte, anti-corrosion, weather-resistant, artistic and high-decorative powder coatings.
