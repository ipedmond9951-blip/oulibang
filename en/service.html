FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I choose the right epoxy resin model?

▼

Choosing epoxy resin mainly considers: ① Application field (coatings, adhesives, casting, etc.); ② Performance requirements (temperature resistance, flexibility, transparency, etc.); ③ Processing technology (curing temperature, time, etc.). We recommend contacting our technical consultant with your specific requirements and we will recommend the most suitable product model.

Q: What is the shelf life of epoxy resin? How should it be stored?

▼

Epoxy resin has a shelf life of 12 months when stored sealed in a cool, dry place. Storage temperature should be controlled below 25°C, avoid direct sunlight and rain. Check product appearance before use — do not use if clumping or abnormalities are found.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity?

▼

Our standard products have a minimum order quantity of 1 ton. For special specifications or custom products, the MOQ may vary. Sample orders are not limited by this policy. Contact our sales manager for details.

Q: What is the typical delivery cycle?

▼

For standard products in stock, delivery is within 3-5 business days. For custom products or large orders, delivery cycle is generally 7-15 business days. Actual delivery time depends on order quantity and product specifications. Please inform us in advance for urgent needs.

Q: What payment methods do you support?

▼

We support multiple payment methods: ① Bank transfer (corporate account); ② Alipay/WeChat Pay (small amounts); ③ Letter of Credit (L/C, for international trade). For long-term clients, we offer monthly settlement. Specific terms can be negotiated with our sales manager.
