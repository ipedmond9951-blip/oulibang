About Us

Quality First · Integrity Forever

Shandong Olibang was established in 2023, is a high-tech enterprise specializing in R&D, production, and sales of epoxy resin. Located in Shandong Province, the company operates modern production workshops and advanced testing equipment, with an annual capacity of 20,000 tons.

Our main products include: epoxy resin series and more, widely used in anti-corrosion coatings, construction materials, composite materials, and electronic insulation. We always focus on customer needs, providing high-quality products and technical services.
